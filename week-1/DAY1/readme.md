
# Day 1 - CRM Basics

## 1. What is CRM?

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.

It is a system used by companies to manage customer information, sales, communication, and business processes in one place.

CRM helps businesses:
- Store customer details
- Track sales
- Improve customer relationships
- Manage business workflow efficiently

Salesforce is one of the most popular CRM platforms used worldwide.

---

## 2. Why Companies Use Salesforce

Companies use Salesforce because it helps them:
- Manage customers easily
- Track leads and sales opportunities
- Improve communication with customers
- Automate business processes
- Increase sales and productivity
- Store all business data securely in the cloud

Salesforce is useful for sales teams, customer support, marketing, hospitals, colleges, and many other industries.

---

## 3. Important Salesforce Concepts

### Account
An Account represents a company or organization.

Example:
- ABC Technologies
- XYZ Hospital
- Sunshine College

---

### Contact
A Contact is a person associated with an account.

Example:
- Student
- Customer
- Employee
- Patient

---

### Opportunity
An Opportunity represents a potential business deal or sale.

Example:
- Student admission process
- Patient treatment package
- Event sponsorship deal

---

## 4. Business Flow

### Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer

1. Lead
A person who shows interest in a product or service.

2. Contact
When the lead information becomes verified, they are stored as a contact.

3. Opportunity
A possible business deal is created.

4. Customer
When the deal is successful, the person becomes a customer.

---

## 5. Real-World Mapping (College Admission System)

### Account
College or University

### Contact
Student

### Lead
Student who is interested in admission

### Opportunity
Admission process or course enrollment

---

## 6. Why CRM is Important in Real Life

CRM helps organizations:
- Maintain records properly
- Communicate better with customers/students/patients
- Track progress of deals or admissions
- Improve customer satisfaction
- Save time through automation
